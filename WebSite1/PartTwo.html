Part two

Educadamente, Seamus saludó al Leprechaun,
y tras varios minutos de conversación,
Seamus se puso impaciente.
Agarró al Leprechaun y le preguntó
dónde tenía el oro escondido.

"¡Está bien!¡Está bien!"
gritó el hombrecito.
"Está cerca de aquí. Te indicaré"

Juntos recorrieron los campos
mientras Seamus no apartaba la vista
del hombrecillo que le guiaba.
Finalmente llegaron a un dorado campo de trigo.

Seamus greeted the leprechaun politely,
And after a few minutes of conversation,
Seamus became impatient.
He grabbed the leprechaun and asked him
where the gold was hidden.

" All right! All right! "
cried the little man.
"It is near here. I'll show you."

Together they set off across the fields
as Seamus didn't take his eyes off
the little man who was guiding him.
Finally they came to a field of golden wheat.
