Part Three

The leprechaun pointed to a big plant.
"The gold is under here," he said.
"All you have to do is to dig down and find it."
Seamus didn't have anything to use for digging,
but he was not entirely stupid.
He pulled of his red neckerchief
and tied it to the plant
so that he would recognize it again.

"Promise me," he said to the leprechaun,
"that you will not untie the neckerchief."
The little man promised faithfully.

El Leprechaun señaló a una gran planta.
"El oro está aquí debajo," dijo.
"Todo lo que tienes que hacer es cavar y encontrarlo."
Seamus no tenía con qué cavar,
pero no era tan estúpido.
Se quitó su pañuelo rojo
y lo ató a la planta
para así reconocerlo de nuevo.

"Prometeme," le dijo al Leprechaun,
"que no desatarás el pañuelo."
El hombrecillo fielmente prometió.
